About us

Our AI based system,provides cutting-edge technology solution by implementing smart irrigation solution in agriculture. It is an Artificial Intelligence based technique, which collects data from various sources and technologies such as Internet-of-Things (smart sensors), multi-spectral remote sensing, Evapotranspiration, vegetation indices(NDVI, EVI, NDWI), water depth, cropping pattern and micro-weather data to arrive at accurate irrigation scheduling. These methods have its own pros and cons, however, in combination they have capability to reach 90-95% accuracy for scheduling farm irrigation.
